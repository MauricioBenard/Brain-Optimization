Gamma Brain Waves

The Gamma Brain Waves link information from all different parts of the brain. These waves show up each time each time you that you completely focused and trying to solve a complex problem.

One way of increasing your Gamma Brain Waves is the meditating from 15 till 20 minutes, 5 days per week.

The researchers at the University of Wisconsin have found that during meditation, Zen Buddhist monks show an extraordinary synchronization of Gamma Brainwaves.

Other useful way is to listen some Gamma videos or playlists, while studying or solving complex problems, requiring a 2nd brain. There are some interesting youtube channels, such as Greenred Productions.

Mozart Power Sounds

Have you ever felt frustrated because you cannot be a baby no more so your mother can put you Mozart and get the different benefits in terms of brain activity?

I have a reconfortating news for you.Researchers from the University of California, Irvine, conduced an investigation on how listening to Mozart affected some cognitive functions and spatial-temporal reasoning on different adults.

The result was an improvement of 3 points in IQ.

Learn to know How To Think instead of What to Think

One powerful technique is the visualization. Think about one goal that you would dream on achieving. Then, close your eyes and contemplate your self at the exact moment, when you reach this goal and how does it makes you feel.

Invocating an specific event, can materialize it.

There are multiple techniques, methods and paths that can potencialize your mind and they just require that you open yourself to the dreamed moment.
